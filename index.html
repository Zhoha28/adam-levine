About

Adam Noah Levine is an American musician, singer, songwriter, record producer, actor and lead vocalist of the pop rock band Maroon 5. Levine began his musical career in 1994 with the band Kara's Flowers, of which he was the lead vocalist and guitarists.

He went on to serve as a judge/coach in a reality show and had some of the greatest singers of all time in his team as part of the reality show. He has starred in a horror television series and has acted in films as well.
